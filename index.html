The process · 03

Five steps.
Zero burden on your team.

From onboarding to removal — we handle every detail. The only decision you make is the one you want to make.

— Before the event
01

Alignment & onboarding

A specialist guides you through pod count, schedule, floor plan, and branding. Returning clients use saved preferences for faster setup.

02

Design & booking setup

Pod branding reviewed and approved. Booking page configured with your logos, colors, and time slots. Branded QR code ready before event day.

03

Logistics & install

We coordinate delivery, venue load-in, and full pod assembly. You provide one 5 Amp outlet per pod — we handle everything else.

— During & after
04

Live support + dashboard

Real-time dashboard shows occupancy, alerts, and bookings. Remote updates pushed to pods instantly. Live support throughout.

05

Removal & lead report

Complete pod removal post-event. You receive a detailed utilization report — bookings, occupancy, and full lead data: names, companies, emails, phones.
