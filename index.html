FAQ

Common questions.
Direct answers.

Everything you need to know before your first deployment — or your fiftieth.

How far in advance should I book event pods?

Book 4–6 weeks before your event for standard configurations. For 10+ pods or peak-season events (CES, SEMA, Dreamforce), contact us 8–12 weeks in advance to secure inventory.

Do event pods require special venue infrastructure?

No. ZenSpace pods require only a standard 110V / 5-amp power outlet and a flat floor surface. No hardwired circuits, no build-out, no construction crew needed.

Can pods be branded with our company logo?

Yes — all exterior panels, glass surfaces, and digital screens become branding real estate. Custom branding packages start at $200 per graphic panel, produced and installed before shipment.

Are ZenSpace pods ADA-compliant?

Yes. The Element ADA Pod offers wider doorways, an ADAAG-compliant layout, and the full 15+ SmartPod feature set. All enclosed pods are UL-certified. ZenSpace recommends at least one ADA pod per 5-unit configuration.

What's the difference between a ZenSpace pod and a standard trade show booth?

A ZenSpace SmartPod provides certified acoustic privacy (−29dB), integrated smart technology, pre-schedulable digital bookings, and built-in lead capture. Exhibitors average 2.4× more qualified lead scans than standard booth configurations.

Do you offer multi-event or annual contracts?

Yes. Organizers running multiple events per year can negotiate annual contracts with preferred pricing — typically 15–25% below standard rates, plus priority fleet scheduling and dedicated account management.

Which cities does ZenSpace serve?

Las Vegas (CES, SEMA, NAB Show), Orlando (OCCC), San Francisco Bay Area (Dreamforce, RSA, Google Next), New York (Javits Center), and nationwide on a case-by-case basis.

How do attendees book a pod at my event?

Attendees self-book via QR code on-site or a direct booking link — no app download required. Pods can also integrate into your event mobile app. Organizers see real-time bookings and utilization in their dashboard.
