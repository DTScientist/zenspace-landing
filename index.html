FAQ · 08

Common questions.
Direct answers.

Everything you need to know before your first deployment — or your fiftieth.

How far in advance should I book event pods?

Book 4–6 weeks before your event for standard configurations of 1–5 pods. For 10+ pods, peak-season events (CES in January, SEMA in November, Dreamforce in September), or custom branding, book 8–12 weeks ahead. Fleet availability is first-confirmed basis.

Do event pods require special venue infrastructure?

No. ZenSpace pods require only a standard 110V power outlet and flat floor surface. No hardwired power, special circuits, or raised flooring needed. ZenSpace coordinates all logistics directly with venue operations.

Can pods be branded with our company logo?

Yes. Full custom branding on windows, walls, doors, table wraps, and exterior digital screens. Starting at $200 per graphic panel. Artwork produced and installed pre-shipment — no on-site setup from your team.

Are ZenSpace pods ADA-compliant?

Yes. The Element ADA Pod features wider doorways and accessible interior layout to ADAAG standards. All enclosed pods are UL-certified. ZenSpace recommends at least one ADA pod per 5-unit configuration.

What's the difference between a ZenSpace pod and a standard trade show booth?

ZenSpace SmartPods provide acoustic privacy (-29dB), integrated smart technology, pre-schedulable digital bookings, and built-in analytics — none of which are standard in traditional booths. Exhibitors average 2.4× more qualified lead scans per day. Trade-off: ZenSpace pods are a premium investment best suited for high-volume meeting objectives.

Do you offer multi-event or annual contracts?

Yes. Annual contracts available for organizers and venues: 15–25% below standard rates, priority fleet scheduling, dedicated account management.

Which cities does ZenSpace serve?

Primary markets: Las Vegas, Orlando, San Francisco Bay Area, New York. We support events nationwide.

How do attendees book a pod at my event?

Self-book via QR code or event app integration — no app download required. The ZenSpace cloud platform is pre-loaded and branded per event. Organizers receive a real-time utilization dashboard throughout.
